What happens if a pick loses? ▼

We mark it as a loss immediately and post a full breakdown of what went wrong. No hiding, no excuses. Our public record is right there on the page — wins and losses both. Long-term edge is the only metric that matters, and that requires complete honesty.

How do pick alerts work? ▼

Subscribe below with your email. The moment a pick is posted, you'll get a notification before the public. Lines move fast — members who act in the first 15 minutes consistently get better numbers. Annual members also receive SMS alerts for immediate action.

Which sportsbooks should I use? ▼

We recommend having accounts at a minimum of three books — DraftKings, FanDuel, and either BetMGM or Caesars. Our picks include the book we found the best number on, but you should always shop. A half-point on a key number is worth more than any edge we can give you.
